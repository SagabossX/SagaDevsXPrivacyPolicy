10. Account and Data Deletion

Our games may allow users to sign in using Google Play Games Services. We do not create or manage user accounts on our own servers and do not store personal user data directly.

If you wish to delete your data associated with our games, you can do so through your Google account:

  • Manage or delete your Google Play Games profile: https://play.google.com/games/profile
  • Manage or delete your Google account: https://myaccount.google.com/
  • Reset your advertising ID: https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/answer/6048248

If you need additional assistance, you may contact us at sagadevsx@gmail.com and we will help guide you through the process.
